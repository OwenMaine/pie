Our Story

Crafting Pizza, Building Community

How It All Started

A Passion for Perfect Pizza

Founded in New York, Creative Food Group, LLC, saw a gap in the fast-casual market. People wanted the quality and customization of a traditional pizzeria but with the speed and convenience required by a modern lifestyle. Pie Five Pizza was born from this vision.

We started with a single shop in Manhattan, armed with a revolutionary oven and a commitment to fresh ingredients. Our concept was simple: empower customers to design their own pizza with unlimited toppings for one flat price, all ready in just five minutes. The idea caught on, and our journey of growth began.

Our Mission

To deliver an unparalleled fast-casual pizza experience by combining speed, quality ingredients, and limitless customization, ensuring every guest leaves happy and satisfied.

Our Vision

To become the nation's leading fast-casual pizza brand, known for innovation, community engagement, and a workplace culture that celebrates creativity and passion.

Franchise & Growth

Join the Pie Five Family

From a single store in New York, Pie Five is rapidly expanding across the country. Our franchise model is built on partnership, providing entrepreneurs with the tools, training, and support needed to run a successful business. We are proud to have over 50 locations nationwide, with plans to double that number in the next three years.
